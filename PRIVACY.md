Our extension requests **minimal permissions** required for functionality:

### 1. Storage
- **Purpose:** Save your settings and stats locally on your device

- **Where:** Only on your device using chrome.storage.local

### 2. Alarms
- **Purpose:** Required for timer functionality (Chrome Extension Manifest V3)
- **What it does:** Creates alarms that trigger every minute to update the timer

### 3. Notifications
- **Purpose:** Alert you when work sessions or breaks complete
- **What we show:** Local notifications with session completion messages
